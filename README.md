# Romasa Qasim — Computational Biology Portfolio

A personal website showcasing research in computational oncology, machine learning for tumor growth prediction, and mathematical modeling of biological systems.

## 🧬 About

This repository hosts the source code for my academic portfolio. I am a PhD candidate at the University of Texas at El Paso (UTEP) working in the Bouchnita Lab. My research focuses on developing mechanistic and machine-learning models to understand cancer evolution and therapeutic response.

## 🚀 Key Projects

### Cancer3D
A 3D hybrid mechanistic model of tumor growth combining:
- **Continuous phase**: PDEs for nutrient diffusion and average cell density
- **Discrete phase**: Stochastic agent-based system tracking individual cell fate (proliferation, migration, apoptosis)
- **Validation**: Tested against *in vitro* growth curves across receptor density conditions
- **Tech**: C++, Python, Agent-based modeling

### ML for Tumor Growth Prediction
Supervised learning models trained on tumor kinetics data to predict growth trajectories and therapeutic response, combining classical regression with deep learning.
- **Tech**: Python, PyTorch, Scikit-learn

### Computational Models in Biological Systems
Broader applications of mathematical modeling to biological phenomena including population dynamics and signaling networks.
- **Tech**: R, Python, Systems biology
